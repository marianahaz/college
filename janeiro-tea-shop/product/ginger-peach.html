About White Ginger Peach

Hydrate from the inside out with a modern melange of invigorating ginger & flavourfully ripe peaches. Sweet yet sharp, this super-smooth iced tea smells as good as it tastes.

What makes it great

  • Perfect iced or even hot.
  • With only 1 g of sugar per cup, it's a great alternative to sugary juices.
  • Naturally caffeine-free so you can enjoy it all day long.

Ingredients

Candied ginger (sugar, ginger, citric acid), Apple, Ginger, Hibiscus blossoms, Natural peach flavouring, Peach.
