About Rooibos Pumpkin Spice

As fall arrives, dip your toes in this delicate and approachable pumpkin blend with only a touch of spice.

What makes it great

  • Light spice and soft pumpkin gives you fall vibes without overpowering your palate.
  • Great alternative to Pumpkin Chai - a bit less intense, while serving all your fave fall time flavours.

Ingredients

Apple, Coconut rasp, White chocolate (sugar, cocoa butter, whole milk powder, sunflower lecithin), Rooibos, Carrot, Natural (pumpkin pie, white chocolate) flavouring, Bean peel, Pumpkin, Pumpkin seed, Sea buckthorn, Stevia extract.
